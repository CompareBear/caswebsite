Badminton is played on a Badminton Court. A typical Badminton Court measures 13.4 metres long, and 6.1 metres wide. The lines marking out the court are different when playing singles or doubles, as shown in the image below.

When playing Badminton, it is particularly useful to get a sense of how big and wide the court is, as this can save you from hitting the shuttle out and losing a point.
